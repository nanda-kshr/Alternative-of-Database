About Map Academy



Your Child's Learning Journey Begins Here








Our Approach


At Map Academy, we believe that every child is unique and has their own way of learning. Our practical educational approach is designed to help every child reach their full potential. We believe that learning should be fun and engaging, which is why we incorporate games and other fun activities into our curriculum. Our experienced and dedicated team is committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for your child to learn and grow. We understand the importance of building a strong foundation for your child's future, which is why we focus on developing their social, emotional, and academic skills.




Health & Safety


At Map Academy, the health and safety of your child is our top priority. We maintain a clean and hygienic environment to minimize the risk of illness and infection. Our facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art security systems to ensure the safety of your child at all times. We also have a team of trained staff who are certified in first aid and CPR, so you can have peace of mind knowing that your child is in good hands.




Our Director


Supraja Pothukuchi, a mother of two and wife of a Microsoft professional, holds a microbiology degree from Tirumala, India. Relocating to Seattle in 2002, she discovered her passion for working with children, volunteering in various activities as her own kids entered school. After a brief stint as a software engineer, she transitioned to education, initiating after-school care in 2017. As a Paraprofessional in Lake Washington School District, she focused on children with disabilities, inspiring her nonprofit vision. Managing a preschool and daycare, her goal is to support all children in need, emphasizing a holistic approach to growth and well-being.
